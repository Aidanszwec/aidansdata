Project Focus

The project focused on the development of a comprehensive dashboard using PowerBI, aimed at providing insightful data related to various job roles across industries. The primary objective was to offer a visual representation of key metrics associated with different professions, enabling stakeholders to gain valuable insights and make informed decisions.


Dashboard Features

Work-Life Balance Metrics: The dashboard featured an analysis of work-life balance across different professions, providing a comparative view of how various roles fared in terms of balancing professional commitments and personal life.

Salary Analysis by Gender: An important aspect of the dashboard was the visualization of salary data based on gender. This analysis aimed to highlight potential disparities and variations in compensation between genders within specific job roles.

Salary Comparison by Job Title: The dashboard included a comparative analysis of salaries across different job titles. This comparison allowed users to identify trends and discrepancies in compensation among various positions within the job market.

Key Objectives Achieved

  • 1. Data Aggregation and Visualization:

    • The project successfully collected, processed, and visualized diverse data sets to create meaningful insights for stakeholders.

  • 2. Insightful Analysis:

    • By presenting key metrics like work-life balance and salary discrepancies, the dashboard provided actionable insights for individuals and organizations to consider.

  • 3. User-Friendly Interface:

    • The dashboard was designed with a user-centric approach, ensuring ease of use and accessibility for a wide range of users, including professionals, HR personnel, and decision-makers.

    Outcome and Impact:

    The project's deliverables facilitated a deeper understanding of the employment landscape, empowering stakeholders to make more informed decisions. By visualizing crucial data points related to work-life balance, gender-based salary discrepancies, and global job trends, the dashboard served as a valuable tool for strategizing and optimizing workforce management strategies.
